ANAMESIS

HOW TO PLAY !!

  • There are 12 pairs of different letter and 1 distinct letter which is odd among all.
  • At max , 2 cards can be REVEALED , which counts 1 TURN .
  • If revealed cards forms a PAIR , it's a SUCCESS , awarding 5 points for each success.
  • For each UNSUCCESSFUL turn , 1 mark will be deducted.
  • If they don't , both cards will be hidden again.
  • BONUS : Identifing that distinct letter will award a bonus points which will be (50 - turns) at that moment.
